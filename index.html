About Capybaras

Capybaras thrive near water sources like rivers, swamps, and wetlands. They are excellent swimmers and can hold their breath underwater for up to 5 minutes!

Fun Facts About Capybaras

  • Capybaras can weigh up to 140 pounds!
  • They are herbivores and mainly eat grass.
  • Capybaras are incredibly friendly and get along well with other animals.
